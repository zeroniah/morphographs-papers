## Why arXiv Instead of SocArXiv?

**Decision**: Switch from SocArXiv → arXiv (January 17, 2026)

**Reasons**:
1. **Computer Science Focus**: VERA validators are CS.AI/CS.CL tools
2. **Broader Audience**: Reaches NLP and legal tech communities
3. **Faster Moderation**: arXiv typically reviews within 1-2 business days
4. **API Access**: arXiv provides better programmatic access to metadata
5. **Tool Integration**: Better alignment with GitHub + Vercel + PythonAnywhere stack

**Categories**:
- cs.AI: Artificial Intelligence (VERA, Counterfactual)
- cs.CL: Computation and Language (DFT, NLP components)
- Secondary: cs.CY (Computers and Society) for legal applications
